A Little Bio

Gina Truong

I was born in Montreal, Quebec where my father's family resides in. My parents were not originally from there, but decided to visit mid July 1983. I guess...I wanted to be with the Canadian culture, and made a decision to come a bit early on July 25th.

I was raised in the states, where I stayed in Los Angeles for a few years until my dad made a decision to bring me to YEE-HAW Texas. Back in the early 1990s, there's basically nothing.

Interests

FOOD - I love everything about food. Food places. Food creativity. Food ideas. Places that ARE worth your time and money. I love hole in the wall restaurants. I love HISTORY.

I am not a big yelper. I do not like to READ a novel about shitty customer service, I don't want to read about if it takes forever or complaints that are not even valid. If the food is GREAT, and their cleanliness is on point, why be petty and go ruin someone's business just because they are trying to accomodate you. If you can sit behind your screen and write a novel, consider what is a true FOOD CRITIC before crying about it.

Travel

Talk about traveling! I enjoy sightseeing, anything that has to do with mountains and lakes. I am in love with Colorado, and I am not big on cities. I already live in a big city, so surrounding myself with beautiful nature is my big getaway.
